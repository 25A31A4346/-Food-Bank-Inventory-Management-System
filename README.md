# 🍱🌍 Food Bank Inventory Management System 🌍🍱

The **Food Bank Inventory Management System** is a C++ based mini project developed to support the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal of **Zero Hunger** 🍞🥗. This project is designed to help food banks, NGOs, charities, community kitchens, and donation centers efficiently manage and distribute food resources to people in need.

In many places around the world, large amounts of food are wasted while millions of people suffer from hunger and malnutrition. This system provides a smart and organized way to manage donated food items, reduce food wastage, and ensure proper food distribution. The application stores important details such as Food ID, Food Name, Quantity, and Expiry Date, making inventory management easier and more efficient.

💡 **Main Objectives of the Project**

* 🍽️ Reduce food wastage
* 🤝 Support hunger relief programs
* 📦 Manage donated food inventory
* 🥫 Track food availability and expiry dates
* 🌍 Promote the Zero Hunger mission
* 📋 Maintain organized food records

✨ **Key Features of the System**

* ➕ Add new food items
* 🔍 Search food records easily
* 📋 View complete inventory details
* ✏️ Update food quantity
* ❌ Delete expired or unavailable items
* 📦 Manage stock efficiently
* 🖥️ User-friendly menu-driven interface

🛠️ **Technologies Used**

* 💻 C++ Programming Language
* 🧠 Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
* 📚 Arrays and Functions
* 🔄 Loops and Conditional Statements

📖 The system works as a digital inventory manager where users can maintain records of food donations and available stock. It helps organizations quickly access food information, monitor inventory levels, and improve food distribution processes.

🌟 **Advantages of this Project**

* Easy to understand and use
* Reduces manual record maintenance
* Saves time in inventory tracking
* Helps avoid food spoilage
* Improves food distribution management
* Encourages social responsibility and hunger awareness
